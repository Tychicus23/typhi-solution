Who We Are

More Than Just
a Cleaning Company

Typhi Solution was founded on the belief that clean spaces foster productive people. We have grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted cleaning service providers, serving homes, offices, schools, hospitals, and event venues.

Our approach is built on three pillars: professionalism, supervision, and hygiene excellence. Every project is managed by experienced supervisors ensuring complete client satisfaction.

We invest heavily in our staff — ongoing training, modern equipment, and eco-friendly products that deliver results without harming the environment.
